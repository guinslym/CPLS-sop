SOP What to Ask Before Going to a Site

Questions to Ask Before Going to a Site: When and what time do you want me to be there? Confirm the exact date and time to arrive on site. What would be my role? Understand the specific responsibilities and tasks assigned. Roles may include: Social Media Photographer/Videographer: Responsible for capturing as many photos and videos as possible. Line Painter: Responsible for painting parking lot lines. Stencil Painter: Responsible for painting any stencils required.
